FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions our clients ask.

How long does account verification take?
Most accounts are verified within 1–2 business days. Once you have submitted your documents, our compliance team will review them and you will receive a notification as soon as your account is approved.
What are the minimum deposit requirements?
The minimum deposit is $100 for a Standard account. You can fund your account via bank transfer, debit/credit card or cryptocurrency — whichever is most convenient for you.
How do I withdraw my funds?
Log in to your trading dashboard, navigate to the Withdraw section, select your method, enter the amount and confirm the request. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours and are typically received within 1–3 business days depending on your payment method.
What platforms can I trade on?
We support the SpeedVerse web platform — which requires no download and works in any browser — as well as TradingView integration, allowing you to trade directly from TradingView's world-class charts using your SpeedVerse credentials.
Is my money safe with SpeedVerse?
Yes. Client funds are held in segregated accounts at tier-1 banks, completely separate from SpeedVerse's own company funds. We are regulated by ASIC, FCA, CIMA and FSC — four of the world's most respected financial authorities.
