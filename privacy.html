Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Last updated: May 2026
SpeedVerse Markets Ltd.
FCA Regulated
Contents
1. Introduction 2. Information We Collect 3. How We Use Your Data 4. Data Sharing 5. Data Retention 6. Security 7. Your Rights 8. Cookies 9. Changes to Policy 10. Contact Us

1Introduction

SpeedVerse Markets Ltd. ("SpeedVerse Markets", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the personal information of our clients, website visitors and prospective clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our website, trading platforms, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy applies to all personal data we process in connection with our Services, and applies to all users regardless of the device or method used to access the Services.

By registering for or using SpeedVerse Markets, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue use of our Services.

2Information We Collect

We collect several categories of personal information, depending on how you interact with us:

  • Identity & KYC data: Full legal name, date of birth, nationality, government-issued identification documents (passport, national ID, driving licence), proof of address, and selfie photograph for identity verification purposes.
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, and country of residence.
  • Account data: Login credentials, account preferences, security settings, and communication preferences.
  • Financial data: Deposit and withdrawal records, transaction history, trading activity, account balances, and source of funds information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, device information, operating system, time zone, pages visited, session duration, and other usage data collected automatically when you access our platform.
  • Communication data: Records of correspondence between you and our support team, including emails, live chat transcripts, and call recordings (where applicable).

We collect this information directly from you, through your use of our Services, and in some cases from third-party service providers (such as identity verification services).

3How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data only where we have a lawful basis to do so. We use your information to:

  • Create, manage, and maintain your trading account
  • Verify your identity and comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulatory requirements
  • Process deposits, withdrawals, and trading transactions
  • Provide customer support and respond to enquiries
  • Send account-related notifications and service updates (e.g., security alerts, policy changes)
  • Send promotional communications where you have consented to receive them
  • Monitor and analyse platform usage to improve our services and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, market abuse, money laundering, and other unlawful or prohibited activities
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations, including those imposed by the FCA, ASIC, CIMA, and FSC
  • Enforce our Terms & Conditions and other applicable agreements

4Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Regulatory authorities: We may disclose your data to the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), ASIC, CIMA, FSC, or other applicable regulators, as required by law or regulatory obligation.
  • Service providers: We engage trusted third-party providers to assist us in delivering our Services, including identity verification providers, payment processors, cloud infrastructure providers, and IT support. These providers are contractually bound to process your data only on our instructions and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose your data to law enforcement agencies, courts, or other government bodies in response to a lawful request, court order, or legal process.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

All third parties with whom we share personal data are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

5Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying any legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements.

In particular:

  • KYC documents and identity verification records are retained for a minimum of 5 years following account closure, as required by anti-money laundering regulations.
  • Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 5 years from the date of the transaction.
  • Account data is retained for the duration of the account relationship and for a period thereafter as required by applicable law.

Once your data is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymise it in accordance with our data retention procedures.

6Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data at rest
  • Strict access controls limiting who can access personal data within our organisation
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) options for account access

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and you use our Services at your own risk.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority in accordance with applicable law.

7Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction and applicable data protection law (including the UK GDPR and EU GDPR), you may have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10. We will respond to your request within 30 days. Note that certain rights may be subject to legal exceptions or limitations.

8Cookies

We use essential cookies and session storage solely to operate our platform — for example, to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics cookies.

Essential cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. By using our platform, you consent to this limited use of cookies.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our Services following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
