Cookie Policy

This policy explains how SpeedVerse Markets uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and trading platforms, and how you can control them.

Last updated: May 2026
SpeedVerse Markets Ltd.
GDPR Compliant

1What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their sites.

Cookies may be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party organisations that provide content, services, or advertising on that website ("third-party cookies"). Cookies can be "session cookies" — which are deleted when you close your browser — or "persistent cookies" — which remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them manually.

In addition to cookies, we may use similar tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage, which function in a similar way to cookies. References to "cookies" in this policy include all such similar technologies unless stated otherwise.

2Why We Use Cookies

SpeedVerse Markets uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To operate our website and trading platforms correctly and securely
  • To maintain your login session and authentication state across page loads
  • To remember your preferences and settings (e.g., language, display preferences)
  • To protect against fraudulent activity, including cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks
  • To understand how visitors navigate our website, so we can improve it
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns (where you have consented)

We only use cookies where necessary for the operation of our platform, or where you have given your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time using the browser controls described in Section 8.

3Types of Cookies We Use

The table below summarises the categories of cookies used on SpeedVerse Markets, their purpose, and their typical retention period.

Category Purpose Retention
Strictly Essential Required for the platform to function. Includes authentication tokens, session identifiers, and security tokens. Session / up to 30 days
Functional Remembers your preferences such as language, display settings, and layout choices. Up to 1 year

4Strictly Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website and trading platform to function. Without them, services such as account login, order submission, and secure data transmission would not be possible. They cannot be disabled.

Examples of strictly essential cookies include:

  • Authentication tokens: Issued when you log in, these identify your session and allow you to navigate between pages without re-entering your credentials.
  • CSRF protection tokens: Short-lived tokens that protect your account against cross-site request forgery attacks.
  • Load balancer cookies: Used to ensure your requests are routed to the correct server within our infrastructure, preventing session drops.
  • User preference cookies: Store platform settings such as your chosen language, chart preferences, and watchlist configuration.

Because these cookies are strictly necessary, they are set automatically when you access our platform and do not require your consent under applicable law. No personal data stored in these cookies is shared with third parties.

5Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website. They help us to understand which pages are most and least popular and how users navigate around the site.

All information collected by analytics cookies is aggregated and therefore anonymous — it does not identify you individually. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site, and will be less able to monitor and improve our services.

Analytics data may be processed by third-party tools operating under data processing agreements, ensuring that your data is handled in accordance with applicable data protection law. Where you are located in the UK or EEA, any international transfer of analytics data is subject to appropriate safeguards.

6Marketing & Advertising Cookies

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display advertisements that are relevant and engaging for the individual user, and thereby more valuable for publishers and third-party advertisers.

Marketing and advertising cookies are only set with your explicit consent. You may withdraw this consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences in your browser settings or by contacting us directly.

If you are an existing client accessing the trading platform, marketing cookies are not set within the authenticated trading environment. They are limited to public-facing marketing pages only.

We do not use cookies to build personal profiles of individual users for sale to third-party data brokers.

7Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that appear on our pages. These third parties may set their own cookies when you visit our website, subject to their own privacy and cookie policies. We do not control these cookies.

Third-party services that may set cookies include:

  • TradingView: When our TradingView-powered charting widgets are embedded in our platform, TradingView may set cookies to remember chart settings and deliver a consistent experience. Please refer to TradingView's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Payment processors: Our payment partners may set cookies to facilitate secure transactions and fraud prevention.
  • Identity verification providers: Third-party KYC/AML verification services may set functional cookies during the verification process.

Where we embed third-party content, we take reasonable steps to ensure those providers meet adequate privacy standards. However, we recommend reviewing each provider's privacy and cookie policy directly.

8Managing & Controlling Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies. The process varies by browser — please refer to your browser's help documentation:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Apple Safari
    • Microsoft Edge
  • Opt-out tools: For analytics and advertising cookies, you can opt out via industry tools such as the Your Online Choices platform (EU/UK) or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool (US).
  • Contact us: You may also contact our team directly to request that certain cookies be disabled or to ask questions about our cookie practices.

Please note that disabling or blocking essential cookies will affect the functionality of our trading platform. If you block essential cookies, some parts of the platform (such as account login) may not work correctly.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use, changes in technology, or changes in applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the platform or by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies. Your continued use of our website following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
