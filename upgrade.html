Frequently Asked Questions
How are account tiers determined?
Account tiers are determined by either your total deposit amount or your 30-day rolling trading volume — whichever threshold you meet first. Your tier is evaluated daily, and upgrades apply automatically within 24 hours of qualifying. Tier downgrade occurs only if you fall below the threshold for 7 consecutive days.
How are trading fees calculated?
Maker fees apply when you place a limit order that goes into the order book and adds liquidity. Taker fees apply when your order is immediately matched against an existing order, removing liquidity. Your tier fee rate is applied to the notional value of each trade. Fees are deducted from your account balance at the time of trade execution.
Can I qualify for Elite by invitation only?
Yes. Elite tier can be accessed either by meeting the financial thresholds ($250,000 minimum deposit or $10M in 30-day volume) or by receiving a direct invitation from our institutional team. Invitations are extended to select high-volume traders, institutional entities, and strategic partners. To express interest, contact us at elite@speedversemarkets.com.
What happens if my volume drops below my current tier?
If your 30-day rolling volume or deposit balance falls below your current tier threshold, you will receive a warning notification. You have a 7-day grace period to restore your qualifying activity. If the threshold is not met within this period, your account will be downgraded to the appropriate tier on the following calendar day. Deposited amounts are evaluated independently and can maintain tier status regardless of trading volume.
